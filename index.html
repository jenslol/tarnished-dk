ABOUT

The name comes from Elden Ring, where "Tarnished" is what they call the ones who lost their grace but kept moving anyway. Something about that stuck with me. Not the fantasy lore — but the implication that losing the pristine state isn't the end. Things break, get deleted, have to be rebuilt. The frustration in the moment always says "it will never be as good". It always is.

I'm based in Haderslev, Denmark. I work in IT at a small company — handling everything from infrastructure to automation to "can you fix my laptop." In the margins I run a homelab, experiment with local AI, and try to make my home smart without giving away my data to do it.

This site exists because I think out loud better in writing than in meetings. It's not a portfolio. It's a log.
